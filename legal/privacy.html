2. What data is collected?

  • Account data: email address, optionally name and profile picture, transmitted via Google Sign-In or email sign-up to Firebase Authentication.
  • Training data: your own plans, exercises, workouts, sets and calendar entries — stored in Cloud Firestore.
  • Health / body data (optional, particularly sensitive): body weight and height, plus self-entered or imported heart-rate and endurance data, only if you enter or import them yourself. These data are subject to special protection under Art. 9 GDPR and are stored exclusively in your private Firestore area. You can edit, leave them blank, or fully delete them at any time via "Delete account".
  • Technical data: device information and error logs that the browser or app shell sends automatically to Firebase.

3. Purpose

Data is processed exclusively to provide the training functionality — in particular cross-device synchronization, saving your sessions, and computing your personal insights (training rhythm, form index, activity calendar).

No sharing with third parties: We do not share your data with third parties — apart from the technical service providers listed under section 5, which are strictly necessary to operate the app.

No ad tracking: ATEM Hybrid does not use any tracking or advertising cookies, no behavioral tracking, and is not connected to any advertising networks. There is no profiling for advertising purposes whatsoever.

4. Legal basis

Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract performance — providing the service requested) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in stable operation of the app). For voluntarily provided health data (body weight/height, heart rate) additionally Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR (explicit consent through active input in the app).

5. Third-party services

We use the following Google Ireland Limited / Google LLC services:

  • Firebase Authentication — account management
  • Cloud Firestore — storage of your training data
  • Firebase Hosting — web-app delivery (if applicable)
  • Google Play / Google Play Billing — app delivery and processing of in-app purchases (if you buy a subscription or licence).

Data may be transferred to the US. Google is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

6. Retention

Your data is kept as long as your account is active. On account deletion, all personal and training data is removed. Backups may persist up to 30 days before final deletion.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • erasure (Art. 17 GDPR) — directly via "Delete account"
  • restriction (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • objection (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • complaint with a supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR)

For requests contact us at [your-email@example.com].

8. Local storage

The app stores settings and cache data exclusively on your device (localStorage, IndexedDB, Service Worker Cache). No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

9. Account & data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time — directly in the app under Profile → Settings → Delete account. This irreversibly removes all training, health and account data. Alternatively, you can request deletion by email to [your-email@example.com].

10. Changes

This policy may be updated. Material changes will be announced in the app.
